Air Fryer Miso-Glazed Salmon

Description

This recipe was inspired by the food blogger TiffyCooks! It is quick, easy and delicious, and if you're a novice cook like me, this recipe is perfect for you!

Prep time: 30 minutes
Cooking time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces skin-on salmon
  • 3 tbsp Japanese mayo
  • 1.5 tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients listed above.
  2. Place the salmon skin-side down in a bowl/container and coat the salmon with the marinade.
  3. Marinate the salmon for at least 30 minutes (ideally overnight).
  4. Place the salmon skin-side down in the air fryer.
  5. Air fry at 205oC for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve over a bed of rice with some steamed bok choy and you have yourself a delicious meal!
